About us

EvacuEase is a web application that helps monitor occupancy and prevent overcrowding. The EvacuEase system collects data such as name, age, and gender. We also ask how many people are in a family going to the evacuation center or if they are going without someone. This data is required so that we can list in and monitor occupancy in the evacuation center.
